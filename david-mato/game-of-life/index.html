conway's game of life
  1. After pressing START, you'll be able to resize the grid with the slider on the right.
  2. Select the cells you want or press R to randomize the grid.
  3. Press Enter ↵ to play the game, S to stop it, and Backspace ← to reset it.
  4. During the game, you can accelerate or decelerate execution by pressing + or -.
